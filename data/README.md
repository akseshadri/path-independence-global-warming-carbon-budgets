# Data Files

Input data files used by the MATLAB scripts.

## EBM Parameters (Paper 1)

- **betaandnu.txt** — Climate feedback parameter (β) and ocean heat uptake efficacy (η) from CMIP5 GCMs (Geoffroy et al. 2013a,b)
- **heatcapacity.txt** — Heat capacity estimates (c\_s, c\_d) for the two-box EBM

## Emissions and Forcing (Both Papers)

- **co2.txt** — Historical annual CO₂ emissions (Global Carbon Project)
- **histF.txt** — Historical total radiative forcing time series

## HFC Data (Paper 2)

- **hfchist.xlsx** — Historical HFC-143a emissions (Simmonds et al. 2017)
- **rcp3proj.xlsx, rcp45proj.xlsx, rcp6proj.xlsx, rcp85proj.xlsx** — RCP scenario projections (van Vuuren et al. 2007; Meinshausen et al. 2011)
